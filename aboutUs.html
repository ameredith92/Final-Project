Cowboy Karaoke With Cowboy Joe

Due to Covid some things have changed, but we have been providing entertainment to the Park City area for over 10 years now!! A whole decade of fun memories and long lasting friendships. Cowboy Joe has provided Karaoke shows to local favorite venues such as The Star Bar, Ciseros, Wing Nutz of Kimbal Junction, Wasatch Brew Pub, Park City Mountain Resort, Rock & Reillys and the Spur Bar. Cowboy Joe can be found at The Cabin every Thursday night at 10 Pm. So grab your friends get dressed up and come out for a night of fun. Lets create some memories together. See you there!
